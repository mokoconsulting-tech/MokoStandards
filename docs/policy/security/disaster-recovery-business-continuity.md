# Disaster Recovery & Business Continuity Policy

**Status**: DRAFT
**Priority**: CRITICAL (Tier 1)
**Owner**: TBD
**Last Updated**: 2026-01-07

## Purpose

This policy defines the disaster recovery and business continuity requirements to ensure the organization can continue operations during and after a disaster or significant disruption.

## Scope

This policy applies to:
- All critical systems and services
- All data and infrastructure
- All personnel involved in recovery operations
- Third-party services and vendors

## Policy Statements

### Recovery Objectives

**To be defined:**
- Recovery Point Objective (RPO) targets
- Recovery Time Objective (RTO) targets
- Maximum Tolerable Downtime (MTD)
- Service-specific objectives

### Recovery Procedures

**To be defined:**
- Step-by-step recovery procedures
- Role assignments and responsibilities
- Recovery site locations
- Communication protocols

### Testing Schedule

**To be defined:**
- Testing frequency
- Test scenarios
- Success criteria
- Documentation requirements

### Communication Plan

**To be defined:**
- Internal communication procedures
- External stakeholder notification
- Media and public relations
- Status update cadence
