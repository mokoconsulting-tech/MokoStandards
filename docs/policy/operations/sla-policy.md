# Service Level Agreement (SLA) Policy

**Status**: DRAFT  
**Priority**: CRITICAL (Tier 1)  
**Owner**: TBD  
**Last Updated**: 2026-01-07

## Purpose

This policy defines the framework for service level agreements to set clear expectations for service availability and performance.

## Scope

This policy applies to:
- All production services
- Internal and external SLAs
- Service providers and vendors
- Support and maintenance activities

## Policy Statements

### Uptime Commitments

**To be defined:**
- Availability targets by service tier
- Planned vs unplanned downtime
- Measurement methodology
- Exclusions and exceptions

### Response Time Guarantees

**To be defined:**
- Response times by severity
- Business hours vs 24/7 coverage
- First response vs resolution time
- Escalation procedures

### Maintenance Windows

**To be defined:**
- Scheduled maintenance windows
- Notification requirements
- Emergency maintenance procedures
- Impact assessment

### Service Credits

**To be defined:**
- Credit calculation methodology
- SLA breach thresholds
- Credit request process
- Maximum credit limits
