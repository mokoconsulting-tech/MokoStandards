## Purpose

This policy establishes a framework for identifying, tracking, and managing technical debt.

## Scope

This policy applies to:
- All software development projects
- Code quality issues
- Architecture and design decisions
- Infrastructure technical debt

## Policy Statements

### Debt Tracking Process

**To be defined:**
- Debt identification methods
- Tracking system and labels
- Debt categorization
- Impact assessment

### Prioritization Criteria

**To be defined:**
- Risk assessment
- Business impact
- Cost of remediation
- Opportunity cost

### Remediation Scheduling

**To be defined:**
- Allocation of sprint capacity
- Debt paydown targets
- Critical debt handling
- Balance with feature work

### Metrics and Reporting

**To be defined:**
- Technical debt ratio
- Debt accumulation rate
- Paydown velocity
- Trend reporting
