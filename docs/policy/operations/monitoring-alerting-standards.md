# Monitoring & Alerting Standards Policy

**Status**: DRAFT
**Priority**: HIGH (Tier 2)
**Owner**: TBD
**Last Updated**: 2026-01-07

## Purpose

This policy establishes standards for monitoring and alerting to ensure system health and rapid incident response.

## Scope

This policy applies to:
- All production systems and services
- Infrastructure and applications
- Performance and availability metrics
- Alert routing and escalation

## Policy Statements

### Metrics to Monitor

**To be defined:**
- Application performance metrics
- Infrastructure metrics (CPU, memory, disk, network)
- Business metrics and KPIs
- Security events and anomalies

### Alert Definitions

**To be defined:**
- Alert severity levels
- Threshold definitions
- Alert conditions and triggers
- Alert suppression and aggregation

### On-Call Procedures

**To be defined:**
- On-call rotation schedule
- Primary and secondary on-call
- Escalation procedures
- Alert acknowledgment requirements

### Dashboard Standards

**To be defined:**
- Dashboard organization
- Key metrics display
- Visualization standards
- Access controls
