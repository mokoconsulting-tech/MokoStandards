## 1. Purpose

The Governance Guide defines the structures, roles, decision‑making processes, and review cadences that ensure the platform and organization operate predictably, transparently, and compliantly.

Governance ensures:

* Consistency of standards
* Clear ownership
* Compliance with legal and security obligations
* Alignment across engineering and operations

## 2. Governance Structure

### 2.1 Governance Committee

Responsible for overseeing policies, standards, and strategic decisions.

Members:

* Governance Lead *(role not created yet)*
* Architecture Lead
* Security Lead *(role not created yet)*
* Operations Lead *(role not created yet)*
* Compliance Lead *(role not created yet)*

### 2.2 Responsibilities

* Approve policies and standards
* Review risk and compliance status
* Ensure consistency across documentation
* Approve major changes to processes or architecture
* Oversee audits and assessments

## 3. Roles & Responsibilities

### Governance Lead *(Not yet created)*

Oversees all governance processes.

### Architecture Lead

Ensures architectural alignment and coherence.

### Security Lead *(Not yet created)*

Owns security policies and architecture.

### Operations Lead *(Not yet created)*

Manages operational standards and on‑call processes.

### Compliance Lead *(Not yet created)*

Maintains compliance obligations, audit trails, and vendor requirements.

## 4. Governance Processes

### 4.1 Policy Management

* Policy creation
* Policy approval
* Annual review
* Version control

### 4.2 Standards Management

Ensures all documentation follows:

* Header standards
* Formatting standards
* Versioning requirements
* Metadata blocks

### 4.3 Audit & Review

Cadence:

* Quarterly governance review
* Annual audit planning
* Post‑incident governance updates

Outputs:

* Updated risk register entries
* Updated compliance documentation
* Required runbooks or policy updates

## 5. Governance Meetings

### 5.1 Weekly Governance Sync

* Review pending RFCs
* Review outstanding changes
* Validate cross‑team blockers

### 5.2 Quarterly Governance Review

* Review risks, audits, roadmaps
* Approve major structural changes
* Update documentation index if needed

### 5.3 Annual Strategic Review

* Long‑term platform planning
* Budget and resourcing recommendations

## 6. Documentation Requirements

Each governance‑related document must include:

* Header with licensing and metadata
* Version history
* Clear ownership
* Linked templates

